# Privacy Policy for Senti

**Last Updated: February 9, 2026**

## Introduction

Senti ("we", "our", or "the app") is an interactive fiction game that respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

**Device Identifier**
- We collect a unique device identifier to enable game rate limiting (5 games per day per device)
- On iOS: We use `identifierForVendor` provided by Apple
- On Android: We use the Android device ID
- This identifier is used locally and is not linked to your personal identity

**Game Data**
- Your created games, game progress, and gameplay choices
- This data is stored locally on your device using secure local storage
- Game state, decision history, and game metadata

**App Settings**
- Language preference (English or Polish)
- Theme preference (Light or Dark mode)
- Nickname (if you choose to set one)
- These preferences are stored locally on your device

### Information You Provide

**User-Generated Content**
- Story prompts and descriptions you enter to create games
- This content is sent to our backend service to generate your personalized game stories
- We do not store personal information in your story prompts

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Game Functionality**: To create and manage your interactive fiction games
2. **Rate Limiting**: To enforce fair usage limits (5 games per day per device)
3. **Translation**: To translate game content between English and Polish using Google Translate API
4. **Personalization**: To save your preferences and game progress locally
5. **App Improvement**: To ensure the app functions properly on your device

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

### Google Translate API
- **Purpose**: Translating game content to Polish
- **Data Shared**: Game text content only (no personal information)
- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Google Fonts
- **Purpose**: Displaying custom fonts in the app
- **Data Shared**: Minimal font request data
- **Privacy Policy**: https://developers.google.com/fonts/faq/privacy

### AWS (Amazon Web Services)
- **Purpose**: Backend API hosting for game generation
- **Data Shared**: Story prompts and device identifiers
- **Privacy Policy**: https://aws.amazon.com/privacy/

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: All game data, preferences, and progress are stored locally on your device using encrypted shared preferences
- **Backend Communication**: Story generation requests are transmitted securely over HTTPS
- **No Cloud Sync**: Your game data is not synchronized to the cloud and remains on your device only
- **Data Retention**: Data persists on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data

## Children's Privacy

Senti does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Access**: All your data is stored locally and accessible through the app
- **Deletion**: You can delete all your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings
- **Opt-Out**: You can choose not to use translation features by keeping the app in English
- **Data Export**: You can manually copy your game stories using the share feature

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your personal information with advertisers
- Track your behavior across other apps or websites
- Collect location data
- Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and on our website

## International Data Transfers

Our backend services are hosted on AWS servers. By using Senti, you consent to the transfer of your story prompts to these servers for game generation purposes.

## Analytics and Tracking

We do **NOT** use:
- Analytics services (no Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
- Advertising tracking
- Third-party cookies
- Behavioral tracking
